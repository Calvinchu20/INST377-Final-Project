This calculates the total intake of food throughout the day and displays the information into a bar graph for comparison to your goals.

Your input should use commas and avoid using the word 'and' to prevent a crash in the Nutritionix API. For example, your input should look like this: bananas, apples, burger, fries, pizza. But not like this: bananas, apples, burger, fries, and pizza.

You can update your daily progress by reentering the form with the new goals and list of food.

You will get a bar graph that displays the comparison as well as text form that show the description of the calculations of calories, fats, carbs, and proteins from the list of food.

All macronutrition are measured in grams.
