FAQs

Question 1: How does this track progress for nutrition?

Answer: This calculates the total intake of food throughout the day and displays the information into a bar graph for comparsion to your goals.

Question 2: How do should I input the list of food?

Answer: Your input should use commas and avoid using the word 'and' to prevent a crash in the Nutritionix API.

For example your input should look like this: bananas,apples,burger,fries,pizza

But not like this: bananas,apples,burger,fries, and pizza.

FAQs

Question 3: How can we update?

Answer: You can update your daily progress by reentering the form with the new goals and list of food.

Question 4: What do we expect from submitting the form?

Answer: You will get a bar graph that display the comparison as well as text form that show the description of the calculations of calories, fats, carbs, and proteins from the list of food.

Question 5: What are the measurement of the carbs, fats, and proteins?"

Answer: All macronutrition are measured in grams.

Step-by-Step Guide

step 1: Enter your first and last name

step 2: Input your goals calories, fats(g), carbs(g), protein(g)

step 3: List the food you ate today and make sure to use "," with no space and dont use and to avoid crashing

step 4: Submit and track your progress
