Introduction

This website is designed to help people learn what nutrients are contained in the food they eat.

By resourcing an English food health dataset provided by nutriotionix, we will search and show you what nutrients your ingredients may contain in comparison to your goals.

Information Problem

Health has been the subject of concern among the citizens of the United States.

The major contributor to this issue is the lack of a healthy diet among citizens.

Many Citizens have struggled to maintain health and it is our job to provide information about improving nutrition

Due to the lack of knowledge on how to maintain a balanced diet and what ingredients to prioritize, people's diets have been on a steady decline.

Features

The website features three pages: Home, About Us and Functionality.

The Home page provides an overview of the website with a mission statement, team introduction and nutrient calculation service.

The About Us page features the team that developed this website, introducing its members and their goal to creating this website.

The Functionality page is the main feature, where users can input their nutrient goals and consumed ingredients to view how close they are to their goals.
